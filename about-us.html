-About georgilas -

Back in the 19th
century, “Georgilas Cave House” was a fortified structure, whose main purpose
was to control and observe the cultivations carried out underneath it.
It was functioning as the local agricultural centre, where the crops got
collected, weighted and packaged to be sold both in the local and in the
national markets. In parallel, “Georgila’s” tight bound with the adjacent old
chapel of “Panaghia Ataliotissa” reveals the ancient – greek connection among
faith to god, family and nature!

Today, “Georgilas” remains not only an impressive construction, but also a unique
destination for immersing into tranquility, peacefulness, privacy and
comfort!
