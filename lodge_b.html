-PERISTERONAS FOLK HOUSE-

White-washed rural apartment (4 sleeps)

"Peristeronas" is a fully autonomous guesthouse aged since the late 19th century. It was named after the hand-made cycladic columbarium built on its rooftop. Today, this “peristeronas” (the greek word for columbarium) is considered of extreme rarity in the entire island, and it offers shelter to small birds as to lay their eggs as well as to emigrant birds in the harvest season.

More about Peristerona

-PERISTERONAS FOLK HOUSE-

"Peristeronas" comprises a spacious living room, with a fireplace and two built comfortable divans, and a separate room with double bed and embedded wash-basin. It also features an equipped kitchen, a built bathroom and private indoor and outdoor sitting/dining area. The decoration is mainly rustic with antiques and handmade furniture. Guests’ are finally able to gaze the green Valley of Eggares and the farming village of Galini as well as to hear the ocean’s breeze from the house’s rooftop.
